About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at GRAVITY – Física Gravitacional: Teoria i Observació, working within the Institut d'Aplicacions Computacionals de Codi Comunitari (IAC3) at the Universitat de les Illes Balears. I am also a member of the LIGO–Virgo–KAGRA Scientific Collaboration.
